# Roger Video Call Video

Place your video file here:

- `roger-video-call.mp4` - Video loop of Roger's face close to camera

## Video Requirements

- Format: MP4
- Duration: 5-10 seconds (will loop)
- Content: A person (preferably with mustache and glasses) getting very close to the camera, saying something like "Allô? Vous me voyez?? L'écran est noir chez moi!!!"

## Free Resources

You can:
1. Record a short video with a team member
2. Use a stock video from pexels.com or pixabay.com
3. Create an animated version

The app will show a placeholder message if the video is not found.
