# Roger Audio Files

Place your audio files here:

- `icq-notification.mp3` - ICQ "uh-oh" notification sound
- `keyboard-click.mp3` - Mechanical keyboard typing sound

## Free Resources

You can find free ICQ sounds and keyboard sounds from:
- freesound.org
- zapsplat.com
- mixkit.co

The app will work without these files (using fallback sounds), but they enhance the experience!
